About Me

The Man Behind the Recipes

A Decade of Culinary Excellence

Hello! I'm Joseph T T. With over 11 years of experience.

My journey began with a BSc in Hotel Management and Catering Science from Bharathiar University. I have served in high-pressure kitchens, including a significant tenure at Nesto Hypermarket in Kerala, and specialized roles aboard international vessels like the MT Sarveshwar.

My Mission

My mission is to bridge the gap between professional restaurant techniques and everyday home cooking. I created Chef Joseph's Kitchen to share the precise timing and professional secrets that I’ve mastered over 11 years in the hospitality industry. My goal is to empower you with the skills and curated tools needed to bring authentic, high-quality flavors to your own table with confidence.
